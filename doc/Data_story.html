Correlation between different schools of thought

Ethan Xu

In philosophy, different people have different opinions. Schools of thought is the perspective of a group of people who share the same opinions. However, I wondered how similar different schools of thought would be. Would Plato's school of thought have more similarity with Aristotle's school of thought than others since Aristotle was Plato's student?

In this project, I used data containing over 300,000 sentences from over 50 texts spanning 10 major schools of philosophy. The represented schools are: Plato, Aristotle, Rationalism, Empiricism, German Idealism, Communism, Capitalism, Phenomenology, Continental Philosophy, and Analytic Philosophy.
